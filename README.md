# 🏎️ Albid Racing

A fast-paced 3D racing game developed in **Unreal Engine**, where players race through exciting tracks, test their driving skills, and compete for the best times. The project focuses on vehicle control, immersive gameplay, responsive driving mechanics, and a polished racing experience.

---

## 🎮 Game Overview

Albid Racing is designed to deliver an enjoyable arcade-style racing experience with smooth vehicle movement, dynamic environments, and challenging tracks. Players must navigate courses, maintain speed, avoid obstacles, and master vehicle handling to achieve the fastest lap times.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🏎️ Realistic vehicle movement and controls
- 🛣️ Multiple racing tracks and environments
- 🎯 Checkpoint and lap-based racing system
- ⚡ Smooth acceleration, braking, and steering
- 🎥 Dynamic camera system
- 🔊 Sound effects and immersive racing atmosphere
- 🎨 High-quality Unreal Engine visuals
- 🕹️ Arcade-style gameplay suitable for all players

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- **Unreal Engine**
- **Blueprint Visual Scripting**
- **3D Environment Assets**
- **Vehicle Movement System**
- **UI Widgets**
- **Audio System**

---

## 🚗 Gameplay

1. Start the race.
2. Control your vehicle using movement inputs.
3. Drive through checkpoints and complete laps.
4. Avoid crashing into obstacles.
5. Reach the finish line with the fastest possible time.
